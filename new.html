Pamas Mission (Philippine Adventist Medical Aviation Services).

In response to the prayers for missionaries to fill the empty shoes of those who have gone before them, the Peterson’s have decided to answer the call. They will join the Amy of those who carry out the Gospel commission and of whom it is said, “ How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him who brings good news, who proclaims peace, who brings glad tidings of good things, who proclaims salvation, Who says to Zion, ‘Your God reigns!’” Isaiah 52:7 Please join us in praying for them as they embark on this mission. They are giving up the comfortable and “safe” life in exchange for uncertainty and potential trials of all kinds. Pray that they will be strong and courageous in this journey and that the Lord’s hand will continue to lead and direct them. Personally, we have experienced that the joys of ministry outweigh the risks. We have found that in perfect surrender is perfect peace.
